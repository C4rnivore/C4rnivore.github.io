White apartaments
White apartamentsYekaterinburg 2023

Project of a cozy apartment for a young family in Yekaterinburg. In addition to the design, the task was to make a competent layout. It was necessary to create an additional bedroom and preserve the spaciousness of the kitchen-living room.

The interior, inspired by the aesthetics of Japanese houses, is designed in a calm natural range. The main color of the walls is white, which makes the apartment visually more spacious. In support of him, gray-beige, on the walls in the hallway and kitchen. The furniture made of light wood veneer emphasizes the environmental friendliness of the interior and its proximity to nature.

The entrance to the bedroom is through an opening with a sliding high door that divides the spaces. Also, the bedroom is separated from the living room by a wall of glass blocks. This way we do not lose the light from the window, but at the same time we barely see what is happening in the bedroom. The bathroom decoration is in perfect harmony with the rest of the apartment. Porcelain stoneware of a neutral shade, but with texture makes the space concise.

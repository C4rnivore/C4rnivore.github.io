Contemporary comfort
Contemporary comfortWrocław 2025

A weekend apartment is the perfect solution for a relaxing getaway with friends or hosting guests. This very idea inspired the interior design.

We crafted the space in a refined yet accessible style-minimalist, yet full of character. The sleek, contemporary design fills the apartment with warmth, while green accents enhance the harmonious color scheme, creating a visual connection between rooms. Black elements add contrast, balancing the light palette with bold details that deepen the appreciation of the interior’s cohesion.

The palette revolves around natural, timeless materials that bring warmth and tranquility. The noble oak flooring plays a key role, offering both visual and tactile comfort.

The kitchen, dining area, and living room merge into a single open space at the heart of the apartment.

The primary bedroom includes a walk-in closet and a private bathroom. The bathroom is styled in earthy tones with soft lighting, evoking a cozy and soothing ambiance.

The guest bathroom features a contrasting scheme: light plastered walls paired with bold black accents.

This space is harmonious, functional, and stylish—every detail is thoughtfully curated and serves a purpose.
